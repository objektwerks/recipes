12” Pizza Recipe
----------------

**Ingredients**

162 grams water @ 100 degrees, which is 60% hydration.
270 grams flour.
9 grams sea salt ( 2 teaspoons ).
1 gram yeast ( fresh, active or instant ) ( 1/4 teaspoon ).

Dough

Pour 100 ( +- 10 ) degree water into a bowl — and stir in the yeast.
Add half of the flour and mix well until a smooth consistency.
Add the sea salt and continue mixing.
Add the remainder of the flour over one or two rounds.
Knead dough, as required, into ball.

Fermentation

Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
Place dough ball in coverable container; and cover.
Ferment for 2 - 4 hours in immediate room.

Shaping

Pre-heat [ pizza ] oven @ 550+ degrees.
Spread flour on board.
Place dough ball on board; and sprinkle flour on dough ball.
Shape dough into a 12” circle, gently using fingers, hands and/or roller.

Toppings

Place pizza on pizza peel or in a pizza pan; then slide pizza into oven.
Add tomatoes and mozzarella to dough.
Optionally add additional toppings.

Baking

Set oven timer for 6+ minutes. [ optionally set phone timer for visual confirmation ]
Remove pizza from oven; and slice.
