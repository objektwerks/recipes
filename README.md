12” Pizza Recipe
----------------

**Ingredients**

1. 162 grams water @ 100 degrees, @ 60% hydration.
2. 270 grams flour.
3. 9 grams sea salt ( 2 teaspoons ).
4. 1 gram yeast ( fresh, active or instant ) ( 1/4 teaspoon ).

**Dough**

1. Pour 100 degree water ( +- 10 ) into a bowl — then stir in yeast.
2. Add half of the flour and mix well until a smooth consistency.
3. Add the sea salt and continue mixing.
4. Add the remainder of the flour over one or two rounds.
5. Knead dough into ball.

**Fermentation**

1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place dough ball in coverable container; and cover.
3. Ferment for 2 - 4 hours in immediate room.

**Shaping**

1. Pre-heat [ pizza ] oven @ 550+ degrees.
2. Spread flour on board.
3. Place dough ball on board; and sprinkle flour on dough ball.
4. Shape dough into a 12” circle, gently using fingers, hands and/or roller.

**Toppings**

1. Place pizza on pizza peel or in a pizza pan; then slide pizza into oven.
2. Add tomatoes and mozzarella to dough.
3. Optionally add additional toppings.

**Baking**

1. Set oven timer for 6+ minutes. [ optionally set phone timer for visual confirmation ]
2. Remove pizza from oven; and slice.
