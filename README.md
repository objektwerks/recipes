12” Pizza Recipe
----------------
>Basic pizza recipe.

**Ingredients**

1. 162 grams water @ 100 degrees, @ 60% hydration ( 2/3 cup ).
2. 270 grams flour ( 1 and 1/8 cups ).
3. 9 grams sea salt ( 2 teaspoons ).
4. 1 gram yeast ( fresh, active or instant ) ( 1/4 teaspoon ).

**Dough**

1. Pour 100 degree water ( +- 10 ) into a bowl — stir in yeast.
2. Add half of flour - mix well into smooth consistency.
3. Add sea salt - continue mixing.
4. Add remainder of flour - over one or two rounds - continue mixing.
5. Knead dough into ball.

**Fermentation**

1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place dough ball in coverable container; then cover.
3. Ferment for 2 - 4 hours in immediate room.

**Shaping**

1. Spread flour on board.
2. Place dough ball on board; and sprinkle flour on dough ball.
3. Shape dough into 12” circle, gently using fingers, hands and/or roller.
4. Pre-heat [ pizza ] oven @ 550+ degrees.

**Toppings**

1. Add tomatoes and mozzarella.
2. Optionally add extra toppings.

**Baking**

1. Place pizza on pizza peel ***or*** in a pizza pan; then slide pizza into oven.
2. Set oven timer for 3+ minutes. [ optionally set phone timer for visual confirmation ]
3. Remove pizza from oven; and slice.
